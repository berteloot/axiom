# Google Ads RSA (Responsive Search Ads)

Use this skill when generating ad copy for Google Ads Responsive Search Ads.

## Constraints (strict)

- **Headlines:** Maximum 30 characters each, including spaces. Generate exactly 15 unique headlines.
- **Descriptions:** Maximum 90 characters each. Generate exactly 4 unique descriptions.
- Every headline and description must be unique; vary angles, value props, and CTAs.
- Include the primary keyword in at least 2–3 headlines; use keywords naturally in descriptions.
- No generic-only CTAs ("Learn More", "Click Here" by themselves). Be specific to the product or offer.

## Best practices

- Front-load the most important words in headlines.
- Use numbers and specifics where they add value.
- Match the searcher’s intent and the landing page.
- Align with the brand voice and value proposition provided.
