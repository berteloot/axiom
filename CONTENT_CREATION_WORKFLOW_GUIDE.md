# Content Creation Workflow - User Guide

## Overview

The Strategic Content Creation workflow helps you create **publication-ready content** that:
- ‚úÖ **Solves your pain clusters** - Every piece directly addresses your organization's core problems
- ‚úÖ **Uses credible sources** - Only cites reputable sources (consulting firms, industry media, research orgs)
- ‚úÖ **Never makes up facts** - All statistics and claims are either sourced or marked for fact-checking
- ‚úÖ **Aligns with your brand** - Uses your value proposition, differentiators, and use cases
- ‚úÖ **Ready to use** - Complete drafts you can copy, export, and publish immediately

---

## Complete Workflow (7 Steps)

### Step 1: Gap Selection
**What happens:** You select a coverage gap from your strategic matrix (ICP √ó Funnel Stage)

**Value:** Ensures you're creating content that fills actual strategic gaps, not random topics.

---

### Step 2: Trending Topics Discovery
**What happens:** 
- System searches for current industry conversations using Jina AI Search
- **Filters out competitor blogs** - Only uses reputable sources
- Prioritizes: Consulting firms (McKinsey, Deloitte, Gartner), industry media, research organizations
- Returns 3-7 trending topics with strategic insights

**Value:** Your content will reference current trends and cite credible sources, increasing authority and relevance.

**You can:** Review trending topics or skip if not needed.

---

### Step 3: Content Idea Generation
**What happens:**
- AI generates 3-5 strategic content ideas
- Each idea:
  - **Solves your pain clusters** (this is mandatory)
  - Uses your brand identity (value prop, differentiators, use cases)
  - Incorporates trending topics (if discovered)
  - Targets your specific ICP at the right funnel stage

**Value:** You get ready-to-use content concepts that are strategically aligned, not generic ideas.

**You can:** Export all ideas at any time for later reference.

---

### Step 4: Idea Selection
**What happens:** You review and select one idea to develop further

**Value:** Choose the idea that best fits your current content needs.

**You can:** Export all ideas before selecting one.

---

### Step 5: Content Brief Review
**What happens:**
- AI creates a detailed strategic brief with:
  - Section-by-section structure
  - Key messages per section
  - Data points to include
  - Tone and style guidelines
  - Success metrics

**Value:** Ensures the content will be structured correctly and aligned with your strategy before writing.

---

### Step 6: Draft Generation
**What happens:**
- AI generates the complete, publication-ready content
- Includes:
  - Full content following the brief structure
  - **Source citations** for all data/statistics
  - **Fact-check notes** for claims that need verification
  - Word count and reading time

**Value:** You get a complete draft ready to use, with all sources cited and transparency about what needs fact-checking.

---

### Step 7: Draft Review & Export
**What happens:** You review the complete draft with:
- Full content text
- List of sources used (with links)
- Fact-check notes (if any)
- Export options

**You can:**
- ‚úÖ **Copy to clipboard** - Paste directly into your CMS
- ‚úÖ **Export as PDF** - Print-ready format
- ‚úÖ **Export as document** - Text file for editing
- ‚úÖ **Export all ideas** - Get all generated ideas in one file

**Value:** Multiple export formats ensure you can use the content however you need it.

## Key Features

### üîí Source Credibility
- **Never uses competitor blogs** - Automatically filtered out
- **Prioritizes reputable sources:**
  - Consulting firms (McKinsey, Deloitte, PwC, Gartner, Forrester, BCG)

## How to Use

### Starting the Workflow

1. **Go to Dashboard** ‚Üí Click "Critical Gaps" card
2. **Find a coverage gap** ‚Üí Look for gaps marked "No assets"
3. **Click "Create Content"** ‚Üí Starts the workflow with that gap pre-selected

### During the Workflow

- **Review each step** - Understand what's being generated
- **Export ideas anytime** - Don't lose your ideas
- **Select the best idea** - Choose what fits your needs
- **Review the brief** - Ensure structure aligns with your goals
- **Generate the draft** - Get the complete content

### After Generation

- **Review fact-check notes** - Verify any marked claims
- **Check sources** - Click source links to verify
- **Copy or export** - Use the content in your workflow
- **Create another** - Fill more gaps in your matrix

## Technical Details

### Source Filtering
- Competitor domains are automatically excluded
- Only reputable sources are used in content generation
- Source type is classified (consulting, industry_media, research, other)
- All sources are cited with proper attribution

### Fact-Checking
- AI never invents statistics
- Claims without sources are marked for verification
- Generic language used when specific data isn't available
- Transparency about what needs fact-checking

### Export Formats
- **Copy:** Plain text to clipboard
- **PDF:** Print-ready HTML (uses browser print)
- **Document:** Text file download (.txt)
- **All Ideas:** Formatted text with all ideas and context
